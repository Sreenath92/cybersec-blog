About this blog

Cybersecurity awareness should be simple enough to share.

CyberSafe Basics is written by Sreenath V to help people understand everyday cyber risks and take practical action before and after scams.

Why I started this

Many cyber attacks do not begin with advanced hacking. They begin with trust, fear, urgency, or a small mistake. This blog turns those situations into clear awareness guides for families, students, workers, and small teams.

The focus is India-first: UPI fraud, WhatsApp impersonation, fake support calls, digital arrest scams, account hacking, phone safety, and how to report quickly.
