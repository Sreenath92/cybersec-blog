India reporting guide

Report quickly, save evidence, and protect your accounts.

For online financial fraud in India, call 1930 immediately and file a complaint at the official National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal.

1. Call 1930 for financial fraud

Use this for UPI, wallet, card, net banking, loan app, and online payment fraud. Speed matters.

2. File at cybercrime.gov.in

Submit your complaint, upload evidence, and keep the complaint ID for tracking.

Open official portal

3. Contact your bank or payment app

Ask them to block suspicious transactions, freeze affected cards or accounts, and give a reference number.

4. For immediate danger, call 112

Contact local police or the national emergency helpline if there is threat, blackmail, or physical danger.

Evidence checklist

Keep these ready before filing.

  • Screenshots of chats, calls, profiles, payment pages, and error screens.
  • Phone numbers, UPI IDs, email addresses, website links, app names, and social media URLs.
  • Transaction IDs, bank reference numbers, dates, times, and exact amounts.
  • Complaint numbers from bank, payment app, social media platform, or police.
  • Do not delete messages or block the scammer until evidence is saved.
